## EN

### **Clapback Sword**

ALT: Cut-and-Riposte

Malachi's first and permanent companion, a talking sward. It's more interested in teasing his owner rather than helping him, still is a valuable source of information about the plot in the beginning of the game. He can also be treated as some kind of tutor teaching basic game mechanics. He is always sarcastic and stinging, but in a funny and good will way.

### **Barman Absinthrayner**

A bartender who, instead of serving drinks, serves up stories. Each mug of beer is the beginning of a new tale, and every cordial is an intriguing enigma.

### **Hammer Hoaxheart**

He is a blacksmith who claims that his swords can cut through shadows and that his armors are impenetrable. However, in reality, his craftsmanship is as reliable as his promises. His tall tales are matched only by the fragility of his metalwork, making him a dubious figure in the village’s already peculiar cast.

### **Zielarka Zmora** => **Potioneer Puzzlemint**

 She is a mysterious herbalist who peddles potions and elixirs, never revealing their contents. Her customers are left guessing whether they’ve purchased a cure or a curse. Each vial is a riddle, and every concoction a gamble, as she blends her brews with a dash of danger and a pinch of the unknown. Her enigmatic presence casts a shadow of doubt over her remedies, making her both feared and sought after in the village.

### **Miss Information**

**Miss Information** is a character who is the living antithesis of a librarian. Instead of guiding you through a labyrinth of knowledge, she plunges you into the depths of literary chaos. Her knowledge is as vast as it is confounding; each of her utterances is a play on words that can unveil the secrets of the universe or end in a jestful pun. Her favorite pastime is watching as seekers of knowledge lose themselves, trying to unravel the meaning of her words.

### **Lady Jinxington, the Perpetually Unlucky Gambler**

Lady Jinxington, the embodiment of misfortune, has a knack for turning every game of chance into a series of disastrous events. No matter how carefully she strategizes or how lucky the odds may seem, luck has a way of turning against her. It's as if Lady Luck herself takes perverse pleasure in making her life a constant roulette of misadventures. Whether it's a simple game of cards or a high-stakes gamble, Lady Jinxington's presence is a guaranteed invitation for chaos and calamity. Her frequent losses have earned her the reputation of being a walking embodiment of bad luck, a cautionary tale for anyone who dares to test their fortunes against the capricious whims of fate.

### **Professor Melodrama, the Overly Dramatic Wizard**

Professor Melodrama, with a flair for theatrics and an exaggerated sense of self-importance, is an overly dramatic wizard who turns even the simplest spells into grandiose performances. Every wave of his wand is accompanied by booming incantations and melodramatic gestures that border on absurdity. His spellcasting displays are more akin to a one-man show than practical magic. With each incantation, he revels in the dramatic tension, pausing for effect, and punctuating his performances with grandiose flourishes. However, behind the theatrics lies a true mastery of the magical arts, making Professor Melodrama a force to be reckoned with despite his penchant for theatricality. While his melodramatic nature might be exasperating to some, others find entertainment in his over-the-top antics, wondering if he has a spell specifically designed to conjure an audience.

### **Sir Blunderbuss, the Clumsy Knight**

Despite his knightly appearance, Sir Blunderbuss is known throughout the kingdom for his remarkable ability to trip over his own feet and accidentally disarm himself in the midst of battle. His sword swings often miss their mark, causing unintended chaos on the battlefield. Yet, in a strange twist of fate, his clumsy nature occasionally leads to unintentional victories, making him a peculiar but somewhat effective hero.

### **Madame Sarcasmia, the Witty Sorceress**

A sorceress with a sharp tongue and an affinity for biting sarcasm, Madame Sarcasmia possesses a spellbook filled with spells that mix magical prowess with snarky remarks. She can conjure fireballs with a sassy quip and summon gusts of wind with a cutting remark. While her magic is potent, her sarcastic nature often lands her in trouble, provoking enemies and irritating allies. Nevertheless, her caustic humor brings a unique blend of wit and magic to the adventure.

### **Captain Quipbeard the Punning Pirate**

With a peg leg, an eyepatch, and an endless arsenal of puns, Captain Quipbeard sails the treacherous seas of hilarity. His pirate crew is skilled in the art of pun-based combat, engaging enemies with rapier-like wit and groan-inducing jokes. While their puns may not be the deadliest weapon, their opponents are often left dazed and confused, unable to fathom the audacity of their wordplay. Captain Quipbeard's ship, "The Pun-dering," is a feared vessel, sailing the seas in search of laughter and booty.

### **Lady Hilarity the Prankster Rogue**

Lady Hilarity is a mischievous rogue who finds joy in playing pranks on unsuspecting adventurers. With her nimble fingers and clever disguises, she thrives on creating chaos and confusion. From swapping potions with unexpected results to leaving fake treasure maps, her pranks keep the hero on their toes. While her tricks can be frustrating, her intentions are usually harmless, and her presence adds a lighthearted element of unpredictability to the hero's journey.
