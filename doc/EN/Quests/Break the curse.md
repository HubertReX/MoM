# Break the curse

Main story thread. The umbrella is `manual` - it is closed by content that does
not exist yet (S06/S07, content debt D15), not by a condition.

## Q01_S00_BREAK_THE_CURSE

**Title**: Break the curse

A curse will not lift itself. Someone has to know how, someone has to be able to, and someone - that would be you - has to put the two together.

**Success**: The curse is broken. The sword maintains this was mostly its own doing.

## Q01_S01_LEARN_ABOUT_CURSE

**Title**: Learn more about the curse

Children's tales and bards' ramblings are not enough to act on. In a tavern everything gets said about everyone - you only need to ask the right person about the right thing.

**Success**: The barman knows someone who does potions. Old, blind, lives by the forest, and people occasionally get worse after her remedies. A glowing recommendation.

## Q01_S05_MEET_MADAME_SARCASMIA

**Title**: Meet Madame Sarcasmia

Knowing about a curse is one thing; getting rid of it is an entirely different kettle of fish. Apparently there is someone who dabbles in exactly that - and who apparently thinks very highly of herself.

**Success**: Sarcasmia listened, sighed theatrically, and concluded it is probably just bad luck. She can help, though. For a small favour.
