# What is going on?

Opening chain. The only quest available at the start of a new game.

## S00_WHAT_IS_GOING_ON

**Title**: What is going on?

You woke up in a stable, in a village whose name you would rather not know, and your sword will not stop talking. Apparently that is not normal. Apparently neither is the rest of it.

**Success**: The sword has a diagnosis: this is not plain bad luck, it is a curse. The details are with the locals, and at this hour the locals are in the tavern.
